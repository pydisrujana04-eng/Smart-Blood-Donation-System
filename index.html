Features

Powered by Smart Technology

Built for emergencies. Designed with care. Trusted across Andhra Pradesh.

📍

Real-time GPS Tracking

Live location via Google Maps API. Find nearest available donor in seconds with radius-based geofence search.

🚨

Emergency Alerts

Firebase FCM push notifications broadcast to all nearby donors instantly during critical blood emergencies.

💬

In-App Secure Chat

Twilio-powered encrypted messaging between donors and patients — coordinate safely without sharing personal numbers.

🆔

Aadhaar Verification

Anti-fake-donor e-KYC via DigiLocker. 100% verified donor identities before every donation.

⭐

Rare Blood Group Tagging

Auto-detects rare groups (AB-, B-, O-). Priority alerts and gold recognition badges for rare donors.

🌐

Multilingual — 12 Languages

Google Translate powers real-time switching between Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam and more.

🔬

Eligibility Checker

Validates haemoglobin, last donation gap, age, weight before confirming eligibility — instant result.

📊

Live Dashboard

Real-time stock levels, donor heatmaps, request tracking and emergency analytics for hospitals.

📱

SMS Alerts (Twilio)

Twilio SMS reaches donors without smartphones. Nobody is excluded from the life-saving network.
